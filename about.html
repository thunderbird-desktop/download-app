About Thunder_bird

Our Mission

In a world of fragmented digital communication, Thunder_bird aims to revive the art of meaningful correspondence. We believe in the power of thoughtful, secure, and open communication to bridge gaps and foster genuine connections in our increasingly digital world.

What We Offer

  • Secure messaging platform with end-to-end encryption
  • Open-source email client for transparency and community-driven development
  • Digital letter writing tools to encourage thoughtful communication
  • Smart inbox organization to help you focus on what matters
  • Cross-platform messaging for seamless communication across devices

Our Philosophy

At Thunder_bird, we believe that communication should be:

Secure

Your privacy is paramount. Our encrypted mail ensures your thoughts remain yours.

Thoughtful

We encourage the art of crafting messages with care and intention.

Open

Our open-source apapproachach ensures transparency and community-driven innovation.

Accessible

Communication tools should be available to everyone, everywhere.
